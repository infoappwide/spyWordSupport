Privacy Policy (SpyWords)

Last updated: August 4, 2025

SpyWords ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • We may collect device information, such as a unique device ID (used for gameplay identity).
  • No email address, name, or contact info is collected.
  • Anonymous gameplay activity may be tracked to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable multiplayer game functionality (device ID as identifier)
  • To improve the overall user experience
  • To prevent misuse or abuse of the system

3. Third-Party Services

SpyWords does not use ads or any third-party analytics tools as of now. We may integrate such services in the future with notice in this policy.

4. User Rights

We don’t store any personal data that can identify you. If you have concerns or questions, you may contact: infoappwide@gmail.com

5. Data Security

All device identifiers are stored securely via Firebase and never shared with third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. All changes will be listed with a new effective date.
